Weak/ Damaged / Poor Natural Lashes / Sensitive Eyes
  • Unfortunately, I will not work on clients (especially those who want long/dramatic lashes) if they have weak/damaged/poor natural lashes. We can always pick a shorter / more natural look OR give the clients lashes a break.

    • ** Please book a 15 minute free consultation on my website if you are not sure if your natural lashes are in good condition.**



    **NOTE** I speak from personal experience when I say it is OK to take lash extension breaks. (ideally 2 months using a lash serum) If you have had lashes for a long time please consider taking a break to grow out your natural lashes. Even if you see the best lash artist and follow your aftercare perfectly, it is still very possible that you will see damage to your natural lashes after having them for a long time. As your natural lashes grow, your extensions get farther and farther from the root of the lash. This makes it harder for your natural lashes to support the weight of the extension, which can cause breakage. Rubbing or itching your lashes in your sleep (either with your hands or against your pillow) can also cause damage to natural lashes.
